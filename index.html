Our Story

Sourced by Hand, Carried with Care

Sacred World Market began with a simple idea: the objects sold under the lanterns of a Chiang Mai night market deserve to reach the world with their stories intact.

We travel Northern Thailand ourselves — through the silversmith lanes of Wua Lai silver village, the temple towns of Chiang Rai, and the Karen hill tribe artisan communities near Chiang Dao — choosing each textile, amulet, and carving directly from the people who make them.

Authentic, ethically sourced artisan objects. Thoughtful presentation. A meaningful connection to the places and people behind them.
